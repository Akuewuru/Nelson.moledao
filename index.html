Blockchain & Web3 Glossary (English – Igbo)

Jump to: A B C

A

Airdrop
English: Free distribution of crypto-tokens to users, usually for promotion.
Igbo: Inye ego dijitalụ n’efu dị ka onyinye nye ndị ọrụ.
Algorithm
English: A set of rules or instructions used by blockchain systems to function.
Igbo: Usoro iwu eji eme ka teknụzụ blockchain rụọ ọrụ nke ọma.
AML (Anti‑Money Laundering)
English: Laws designed to prevent the use of crypto for illegal money movement.
Igbo: Iwu e debere iji gbochie iji ego dijitalụ mee ihe ọjọọ ma ọ bụ mee ka ego na-adịghị mma bụrụ nke ọma.
API (Application Programming Interface)
English: A set of protocols that lets different software talk to one another.
Igbo: Usoro e si mee ka mmemme dị iche iche nwee ike ikwurịta okwu.
ASIC Resistant
English: A coin or algorithm designed to block specialized mining hardware.
Igbo: Usoro e si chepụta ego dijitalụ ka akụrụngwa pụrụ iche ghara iji ya mee ihe.
Attestation
English: On-chain proof that a person or object has a given identity or attribute.
Igbo: Ihe àmà na-egosi na mmadụ ma ọ bụ ihe nwere njirimara a kpọrọ aha n'elu blockchain.
ASIC
English: Hardware built solely to mine a particular crypto algorithm.
Igbo: Igwe pụrụ iche e wuru iji kụwaa otu ụdị ego dijitalụ.
Automatic Replay Protection
English: Stops a valid transaction from being reused on another chain.
Igbo: Nchekwa megide mmegharị azụmahịa n'ụzọ na-ezighị ezi n'ugbu a ọzọ.
All-Time-Low (ATL)
English: The lowest price a coin or token has recorded since launch.
Igbo: Ọnụ ahịa kacha ala ego dijitalụ rutere kemgbe ewepụtara ya.
Arbitrum Stylus
English: A WASM-friendly programming environment in Arbitrum Nitro.
Igbo: gburugburu mmemme na-akwado WASM na sistemụ Arbitrum Nitro.
Arbitrum Orbit
English: A tool that helps builders launch Layer-3 chains on Arbitrum.
Igbo: Ngwa eji wuo ma gbapụ Layer-3 blockchain n’elu Arbitrum.
Arbitrum Nova
English: A Layer-2 solution built for high-throughput dApps.
Igbo: Ngwọta Layer-2 wuru maka ngwa dijitalụ nke na-arụ ọrụ ngwa ngwa.
Arbitrum Nitro
English: Upgraded stack powering Arbitrum One.
Igbo: Ngwakọta teknụzụ ọhụrụ na-enye Arbitrum One ike.
Arbitrum One
English: The main roll-up chain in the Arbitrum network.
Igbo: Isi blockchain nke Arbitrum.
Arbitrum
English: A Layer-2 Ethereum scaling solution.
Igbo: Ngwọta mgbatị Ethereum dị n’ogologo Layer-2.
AppChain
English: A blockchain purpose-built for a single application.
Igbo: Blockchain e wuru iji rụọ ọrụ otu ngwa.
ArbOS
English: The software that runs Arbitrum’s Layer-2 protocol.
Igbo: Ngwaọrụ sọftụwia na-achịkwa ọrụ Layer-2 nke Arbitrum.
Address
English: A public key or identifier used for sending and receiving crypto.
Igbo: Koodu ma ọ bụ aha eji eziga ma nata ego dijitalụ.
Actor Model
English: A pattern that treats computation as interacting "actors."
Igbo: Usoro e si ele usoro mmemme anya dịka mmadụ na-arụ ọrụ n'otu n'otu.
Algorithmic Stablecoin
English: A stablecoin maintained by code, not collateral.
Igbo: Ego dijitalụ a na-achịkwa site na koodu kama ịtọ ntọala ego.
Address Delegation
English: When one address gives another authority to act on its behalf.
Igbo: Mgbe adreesị otu onye nyere onye ọzọ ikike ime ihe n’aha ya.
All-Time-High (ATH)
English: The highest price a crypto has ever reached.
Igbo: Ọnụ ahịa kacha elu ego dijitalụ rutere kemgbe e guzobere ya.
Actor
English: Any entity interacting with a blockchain.
Igbo: Onye ma ọ bụ ihe ọ bụla na-arụkọ ọrụ na blockchain.
Arbitrage
English: Profiting from price differences between exchanges.
Igbo: Ịzụ ahịa n'ụlọ ahịa dị ọnụ ala ma ree ya n'ahịa dị elu iji nweta uru.
Altcoin
English: Any cryptocurrency that isn’t Bitcoin.
Igbo: Ego dijitalụ ọ bụla ewezuga Bitcoin.
Atomic SWAP
English: Trustless, automatic crypto exchange across blockchains.
Igbo: Gbanwere ego dijitalụ n’enweghị ntụkwasị obi n’etiti blockchains abụọ.
Account
English: A record that stores token balances and permissions.
Igbo: Akaụntụ nke na-edekọ ego dijitalụ na ikike onye ọrụ.
Air-gapping
English: Keeping a system offline to protect it from hacking.
Igbo: Iche ngwaọrụ site na ịntanetị iji chekwaa ya pụọ n'aka ndị ohi dijitalụ.
Allowlist
English: A list of approved addresses for specific access.
Igbo: Ndepụta adreesị kwadoro maka mmepe ma ọ bụ ntinye.
Ask Me Anything (AMA)
English: An open Q&A session with a project team.
Igbo: Oge ajụjụ na azịza onye ọ bụla nwere ike ịjụ ndị ọrụ oru.
Anchor
English: A Solana framework for building smart contracts.
Igbo: Usoro a na-eji wuo nkwekọrịta ọgụgụ isi na Solana.
API Node
English: A blockchain node accessed via APIs.
Igbo: Ọkpụkpụ blockchain a na-enweta site na API.
Address-Owned Object
English: In Sui, an object owned by an address.
Igbo: N’ime Sui, ihe ọ bụla e jikọtara na adreesị onye nwe ya.
Account Abstraction
English: A system where smart contract wallets act like regular accounts.
Igbo: Usoro nke na-eme ka akpa ego nkwekọrịta ọgụgụ isi nwee àgwà dị ka akaụntụ nkịtị.
Abstracted Interoperability
English: Simplified cross-chain communication.
Igbo: Ịmekọrịta n’etiti blockchains n’enweghị mgbagwoju anya.
